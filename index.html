The Problem

Sound familiar?

Millions of Nigerian traders lose money every month, not because customers won't pay, but because records get lost and follow-ups slip through the cracks.

📒
Your paper book gets lost

Paper records disappear, get damaged by rain, or become unreadable. When the book is gone, the money is gone, and there's nothing you can do about it.

📱
WhatsApp messages get buried

Chasing customers through long WhatsApp threads is slow, unprofessional, and easy for customers to ignore. You spend more time searching than collecting.

🤔
You forget who owes what

With dozens of credit customers, it's impossible to remember every amount and every due date. Money slips through the cracks, sometimes for months.

Features

Everything you need to run your credit business professionally

Professional Receipts
